The Problem

We discovered that finding a collective list of specific volunteer opportunities is difficult because many opportunities remain unreported.

Our Solution

This easy-to-use website allows people to search for volunteer/donation opportunities by category. Volunteers who sign in may also log their service hours.

Why

Giving back to the community is crucial! We want to help others quickly find ways to help out in their category of interest.
